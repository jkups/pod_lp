Privacy Policy

Last updated: 29/12/2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Odarun (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, discloses, and protects personal information when you access or use odarun.com (the “Website”) and our software-as-a-service print-on-demand platform (the “Service”).

1. About Odarun

Odarun provides a SaaS platform for print-on-demand businesses, enabling users to design, manage, and sell custom-printed products through integrated tools and workflows.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Name and email address
  • Business name and brand details
  • Billing address
  • Uploaded designs, files, and product data
  • Support communications

2.2 Payment Information (Stripe)

Payments are processed securely by Stripe, Inc. Odarun does not store any payment card details. Stripe's data handling is governed by its own privacy policy.

2.3 Automatically Collected Data

  • IP address
  • Browser and device type
  • Operating system
  • Usage and interaction data

2.4 Analytics

We use Microsoft Clarity to understand how users interact with the platform. This may include session recordings, clicks, and navigation behavior. Sensitive fields are masked, and data is used solely to improve usability and performance.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and operate the Odarun platform
  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Process subscriptions and payments
  • Enable print-on-demand workflows
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve features, performance, and security
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Legal Bases for Processing (GDPR)

  • Performance of a contract
  • User consent
  • Legitimate business interests
  • Compliance with legal obligations

5. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to maintain sessions, remember preferences, enable core functionality, and analyze usage. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

6. Sharing of Information

We may share information with:

  • Stripe (payment processing)
  • Microsoft Clarity (analytics)
  • Marketplaces (e.g. Amazon Seller Central)
  • Print Prroviders (e.g. Printful)
  • Cloud hosting and infrastructure providers
  • Customer support tools
  • Legal or regulatory authorities when required

We do not sell personal information.

7. User-Generated Content

Designs, files, and content you upload or create remain your property. We process this content only to provide platform functionality and do not use it for marketing or resale without consent.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be stored or processed outside your country of residence. We apply appropriate safeguards for international data transfers where required.

9. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to provide the Service, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes.

10. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no online service is completely secure.

11. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data, and to withdraw consent.

12. California Privacy Rights (CCPA/CPRA)

California residents have the right to know, delete, and opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information. Odarun does not sell personal data.

13. Children's Privacy

Odarun is not intended for children under 13 (or 16 where applicable). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

14. Third-Party Services

Our Service integrates with third-party tools. We are not responsible for their privacy practices and encourage you to review their policies.

15. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.
