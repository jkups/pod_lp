Affiliate Program Terms and Condition

Welcome to the Odarun Affiliate Program. You can join the program by clicking here.
By signing up to become an Affiliate, you agree to comply with the following terms, conditions, and payout structures.

1. Comission Structure

We reward our affiliates for driving new, paid subscriptions to our platform.

  • Commission Rate: You will earn a 20% commission on the recurring revenue generated by your direct referrals.
  • Duration: Commissions are paid for up to the first 12 months of the user's active, paid subscription.
  • Eligible Plan: Commissions apply to both monthly and annual plans. Commissions are calculated based on the actual price paid by the customer after any discounts are applied.

2. Referral Incentive (The 10% Discount)

To help you convert your audience, your unique affiliate link or coupon code provides an exclusive incentive to your referrals:

  • The Offer: Referrals receive a 10% discount on their subscription.
  • Tier Limitation: This 10% discount applies strictly to the Monthly subscription tier.
  • Exclusion: The discount does not apply to Annual plans or any custom tiers.

3. Cookie Window

  • 60-Day Tracking: Our affiliate tracking cookies last for 60 days.
  • Attribution: If a user clicks your link, they must register an account and subscribe within 60 days for the sale to be attributed to you. We operate on a "last-click" basis.

4. Payout Requirements and Schedule

To protect our business against customer refunds, we operate a strict verification window.

  • Minimum Payout: You must accumulate at least $100 USD in approved commissions before a payout is triggered.
  • Payout Method: All commissions are paid out securely via Wise. You must maintain a valid Wise account to receive funds.
  • Payout Delay (Locking Period): Commissions are marked as "Pending" for 30 days from the date the customer pays their invoice.
  • Payment Date: Approved commissions are processed on the 30th of every month for the previous month's locked balances.

5. Prohibited Promotional Activities

To maintain the integrity of our brand, affiliates are strictly prohibited from:

  • Self-Referrals: You cannot use your own affiliate link to buy a subscription for yourself, your company, or your immediate team.
  • Spamming: Sending unsolicited bulk emails, mass forum postings, or automated social media spam is ground for immediate termination.
  • Misrepresentation: You must not misrepresent our platform’s features, pricing, or guarantees.
  • PPC Trademark Bidding: You may not run Google Ads, Bing Ads, or other paid search traffic using our trademarked name Odarun or variations/misspellings of it.

6. Termination

We reserve the right to terminate any affiliate account at any time if these terms are violated. If an account is banned for fraudulent activity or policy violation, all pending and approved commissions will be forfeited.
